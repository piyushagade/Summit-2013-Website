Summit '12

23 Sep, 2012 at MIT Pune

MAEER's MIT, Pune had organized a National Level Inter-Engineering Sports Meet, "MIT SUMMIT '12" on 28th, 29th, 30th September, 1st & 2nd of October 2012. SUMMIT was held for the 6th time at MIT, Pune. Our meet held 14 disciplines which were Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Cricket, Baseball, Badminton, Table Tennis, Chess, Carrom, Squash, Water polo, Swimming & Rowing.

Over 75 colleges participated in the event with outstation teams coming from major cities like Mumbai, Vishakhapatnam, Bangalore, Chennai, Nashik, Kolhapur, Nanded and Ahmednagar. Satyabhama University, Chennai, Gitam University, Dhirubhai Ambani College of Engineering and NIT Kurukshetra were some of the teams outside Maharashtra that participated. More than 3200 sportspersons participated in the five day meet.

The meet commenced with the Inauguration Ceremony held at MIT ground which was graced by Hon. Mr. Yogeshwar Dutt, Mr. Shreekant Wagh, member of Pune Sahara Warriors cricket team, Lt. Col. J.S.Thind, Trainining Officer, Army Rowing Node, Pune and Subedar R.P Shelke, Asian Gold Medalist.

The winning and runner-up teams were given cash prizes to the tune of Rs. 6 lac. in addition to the attractive trophies and medals. 32 Individual prizes were awarded to the sports stars of each event. Prizes were given at the function held in Dyaneshwara Hall.The overall championship was won by the host team MIT which include a cash prize of Rs. 25,000/- cash prize and a grand trophy.
