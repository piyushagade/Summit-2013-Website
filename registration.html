Registration Info

Instructions

    For colleges in Pune:
  • The registration of all events of your college must be done at one time. Multiple entries from one college will not be accepted.
  • Registration fees should be paid at the MIT gymkhana registration desk.
  • Without the payment of the registration fee your entry will not be considered.
  • Please bring the receipt of the fees paid at the time of reporting.
  • Signing of the undertaking by the sports in charge/secretary is a must without which none of your college teams will be allowed to participate.

    For colleges outside Pune:
  • Please send the registration forms, players list as well as the undertaking signed by the sports in charge/secretary back to MIT.
  • All the copies should reach MIT College at the address given below:

    Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune
    S. No. 124, Ex-servicemen colony, Paud road,
    Kothrud, Pune 411038.
    Tel No.: 020-30273647/020-30273459
    Fax No: +91-20-25442770/30273506
    e-mail: mitsummit@mitpune.edu.in

  • Along with the copies the registration fees should also be
    sent by cash or DD in favour of “Maharashtra Institute of
    Technology, Pune”.
  • The participating teams should send a fax of registration
    form in order to confirm registration.

    • At the time of registration:
  • You will have to send a separate DD of Rs. 5000/- as a deposit in favor of “Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune” as a confirmation of your accommodation. This amount will be returned to you when you are leaving Pune.
  • In case of misbehavior of any kind, MIT reserves the right to take any punitive action, even to the extent of confiscating the security deposit and registration fees, cancelling the participation of the concerned college, escorting them off the campus and sending a report of the misconduct to the head of the concerned institution.
  • Extra accommodation and boarding facilities will be charged as applicable.
