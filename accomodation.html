Accomodation

All the teams participating in Summit '13 coming from outside Pune city will be given accommodation by MIT under the following conditions.

At the time of registration:

1. You will have to send a separate DD of Rs. 5000 /- as a deposit in favor of “Maharashtra Institute of Technology, Pune” as a confirmation of your accommodation. This amount will be returned to you when you are leaving Pune.
2. In case of misbehavior of any kind, MIT reserves the right to take any punitive action, even to the extent of confiscating the security deposit and registration fees, cancelling the participation of the concerned college, escorting them off the campus and sending a report of the misconduct to the head of the concerned institution.
3. Extra accommodation and boarding facilities will be charged as applicable.

On your arrival in Pune:


1. Report at the MIT, Kothrud Campus help desk where you will be informed about your accommodation details.
2. Incase the D.D. has not been given due to any circumstance, you can deposit Rs. 5,000/- in cash, at the help desk, as the security deposit which will be refunded while leaving Pune.
3. MIT will provide you with mattresses and quilts. You need not bring it along.
4. If you have not registered beforehand then MIT will be helpless in finding you accommodation.
5. Per head expenditure of accommodation, food & transport will be Rs. 1,500/- for five days i.e. 20th, 21st, 22nd, 23rd and 24th September, 2013.
6. The teams coming on 15th September should report at MIT Gymkhana before 2 p.m and should be present on the ground at 3:30 p.m. for the Inaugural Ceremony.
7. The lodging will be provided on 15th September free of cost (excluding boarding facility).
8. Apart from this you can make arrangements for your own accommodation as per your convenience.
