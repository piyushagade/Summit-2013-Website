Since the app is not stored on Android market, you will have to enable "Install from unknows sources (Non-market)" in your settings. Follow these steps:
  • After downloading, "summit.apk" from the link above, tranfer the file to your android device and open the file.
  • In the worst case, a dialog box should appear. Click on "Settings" and check "Unkown Sources" checkbox. Hit "Ok".
  • Now open the "summit.apk" again.
