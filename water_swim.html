Swimming & Water Polo

Rules

Swimming:

  • Maximum 2 players are allowed for each category from each college.
  • Event will be conducted only if no. of participants is at least 4 from 3 different colleges.
  • One player can participate in any 3 individual events.
  • No spot entries.

Waterpolo:

  • Maximum no. of players: 13
  • Balls will be provided.
  • Incase of a draw, 2 halves of 3 mins each will be played & if required then penalty shootout will decide the winner.

 

Categories

Swimming :

  • 50/100m Freestyle M/W
  • 50/100m Backstroke M/W.
  • 50/100m Breaststroke M/W
  • 50/100m Butterfly M/W
  • 4X50M Freestyle Relay M/W.
  • 4X50M Medley Relay M/W
